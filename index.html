How we work

We are purely work to raise funds for our charity projects. The company is the subsidiary of Zia Siddiquefoundation, which is a charitable organization and working to uplift ultra poor, poor and vulnerable communities since 2001. DES is established for sustainable and long-term support to those who cannot afford nutritional food, education and health facilities for their children. 100% profit of the company goes to Zia Siddique Foundation and ZSF spent this amount for the well being of deprived communities across Pakistan. We are accountable and reportable before our clients. Our annual audit and financial reports are open for public and any client or non client can demand and we are bound to present these reports.
