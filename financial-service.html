Financial Services in Islamabad

DES is a social enterprise and income generation project of Zia Siddique foundation and pak-emirates poly technique institute and offering a range of solutions. The ultimate goal of DES is to provide high quality janitorial, maintenance, design & construction landscaping, IT and financial services of to our clients in twin cities and spent the profit to provide food, shelter, education and health facilities to marginalized groups especially orphan and widows. We are the first company in market with the idea of serving to all social classes rather than profit maximization.
We have our own pool of engineers, expert workers, supervisors and skilled labor along with advance tools and safety measures in our technical training institute situated in the area of one acre in IJP road Pir Wadai Moor Rawalpindi which is also the operational hub of DES. Our prices are nominal and services are backed by a feedback system through our customer care department. Customer satisfaction through quality services and quick response is our first priority.
We are launching our services through a website and mobile application. Download our application get online quotes and work done by our team within committed time period.
